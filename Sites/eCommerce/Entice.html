Enticing people to use website:

1. Emails

Amazon sends emails to several thousands of computer users randomly. They also send it to many customers regarding great sales and offers. There is usually some interactive content too such as an animation. They also have a link to join Amazon directly in the e-mail.

2. Youtube ads

Almost all videos on youtube nowadays have ads on them. Amazon, being a big sales company makes good use of this advertising medium and places many ads on many videos. This ensures that more people know about Amazon and perhaps, buy products from them in the future. 3. Ads on several websites and blogs

4. TV ads

This is also a medium that is likely to reach many people as almost everyone on the planet has a TV these days. These ads, like the youtube ads, are very short and crisp as they have to convey their message within 30 seconds approximately

5. Google search ads for consumer products

Whenever we search online for something we can buy, there is almost always an ad that links to a online shopping website. More often than not, it's likely to be Amazon.in and there is usually a price mentioned along with related products. This is intended to attract the person to shop online rather than going out to buy them
