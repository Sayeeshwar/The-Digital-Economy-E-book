Purpose and Objectives
As with most websites, Amazon.in uses a username and password system to identify its customers

Amazon has a huge database for all its customer details. When you enter the username and password, it searches its records and checks whether the username matches the password. So, it knows that you are currently using the website.

While paying for your goods, your credit card information is sent to your card company. Here the details are checked whether they are correct and the authentication is sent back to the payment portal. This then allows Amazon to confirm and place the order for your goods delivery.
