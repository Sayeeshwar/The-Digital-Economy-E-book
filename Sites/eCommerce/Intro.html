Purpose and Objectives
Amazon is an online e-commerce company that specialises in selling a variety of products online that can be delivered to your doorstep. It started 20 years ago and was founded by Jeff Bezos.

In its early days, Amazon mainly operated as an online bookstore. Later, it started selling many other products as furniture, toys, jewellery. But a majority of its product range is made of electronics such as DVDs, electronics, house appliances and media content.

Recently, Amazon has started selling consumer electronics of its own with the range of kindle book readers, Fire TVs, Tablets, and phones. It operates in around 15 countries with plans to expand in many more.

To use the service, users need an amazon account. This service has many strengths and weaknesses. These will be looked at in detail throughout this e portfolio.
